The CartaCuba:
A Living Spectrum

The namesake of our brand, the *CartaCuba* (Cuban Tody), is a tiny marvel of the biosphere. Measuring barely 11 centimeters, this iridescent creature embodies the vibrant resilience of Camagüey's woodlands.
